My Top Five Anime of 2021

Jujutsu Kaisen


Yuji Itadori is an expert at track and field. But he has zero interest running around in circles, he's happy as a clam in the Occcult Research Club. Although he's only in the club for kicks, things get serious when a real spirit shows up at school!

When his grandfather is on his deathbed, Yuji makes a promise to him; that he will do kind deeds so that he dies
surrounded by loved ones, unlike his grandfather.

However, Yuji's life takes a sudden turn as he meets Megumi Fushiguro; a jujutsu sorcerer who tries to retrieve a
cursed object. When Yuji's friends open the cursed object that they found, things go south as the cursed spirits that it spawned start roaming in school. As Fushiguro and Yuji are almost defeated by one of the spirits. Yuji then swallows the cursed object and gains its power. As result, Yuji becomes cursed by the object and gets dragged into Fushiguro's thrilling world of jujutsu sorcerers and cursed spirits.


Attack on Titan

"That day, the human race remembered the terror of being dominated by them, and the shame of being held captive in a birdcage..." - Over 100 years ago, a natural predator of humanity appeared: the Titans, giant humanoid but mindless monsters whose sole purpose of existence seemed to be to devour humans. There was an insurmountablr gap in power between them and mankind, and as a result, humanity was rapidly exterminated to the brink of extinction. The survivors responded by constructing three concentric wall: Wall Maria, Wall Rose and Wall Sina, whhich graced them with a century of peace. However, one day a colossal Titan far larger than any other seen before breached the outer wall, allowing the smaller Titans to invade humanity territory and forcing the survivors to retreat to the inner walls. Eren Jeager, a boy whose mother was eaten during the invasion, vowed to wipe every last Titan off the facae of the Earth, and joined the military determined to exact his revenge.


Kimetsu no Yaiba


In Taisho-era Japan, Tanjiro Kamado is a kind hearted boy who makes a living selling charcoal. However, his peaceful life is shattered when a Demon slaughtered his entire family. His little siste Nezuko is the only survivvor, but she has been transformed into a Demon herself! Tanjiro sets out on a dangerous journey to find a way to return his sister to normal and destroy the Demon who ruined his life.


Platinum End

God is retiring, so thirteen angels are sent to each select a candidate from which one will become the next God. Mirai Kakehashi just wanted to die when an angel, Nasse, saves his life. Now a god candidate, Mirai is trying to find happiness, but among the other candidates there are those willing to do anything to become God, including killing the competition.


Weathering with you

Set during a period of exceptionally rainy weather, high-school boy Hodaka Morishima runs away from his troubled rural home to Tokyo and befriends an orphan girl who can manipulate the weather.
